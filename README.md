Contract Analyzer 

An AI-powered contract review tool that helps startups, SMEs, and legal teams quickly identify risks, missing protections, and key obligations in contracts and agreements.

## Overview
*Contract Analyzer* automates contract analysis using Large Language Models (LLMs).  
Upload a contract, and the system highlights:

- Risky clauses
- Missing or weak legal protections
- Key obligations, deadlines, and responsibilities

The goal is to reduce manual legal review time while improving accuracy and accessibility.

## Features

- *Risky Clause Detection*  
  Identifies clauses that may introduce legal or financial risk.

- *Missing Protections Analysis*  
  Flags commonly expected clauses that are absent or insufficient.

- *Key Obligation Extraction*  
  Summarizes responsibilities, deadlines, and deliverables.

- *Semantic Search*  
  Search across contracts using natural language.

- *Contract Summarization*  
  Generates concise summaries for long or complex agreements.

- *Exportable Reports*  
  Download structured analysis for audits or legal review.


## How It Works

1. Upload a contract (PDF or DOCX).
2. Text is extracted and split into logical clauses.
3. Clauses are embedded and stored in a vector database.
4. An LLM analyzes each clause for:
   - Risk
   - Missing safeguards
   - Obligations
5. Results are displayed in a structured, readable format.Contract Analyzer
